Welcome to STEM! These courses are designed to introduce you to the fundamentals of engineering.

  • STEM 1: Introduction to Engineering Principles
  • STEM 2: Programming Robotics & Electrical Systems
  • STEM 3: Advanced Programming and Mechanical Concepts

Learning Outcomes

Describe how engineers use a systematic design process to solve problems.
Read and interpret technical drawings and schematics.
Analyze a problem and develop a solution collaboratively using engineering principles.
Read and predict an algorithm's behavior.
Use programming concepts to create a program that solves a problem.
Discuss how people interact with copmuting and its implications for society.
